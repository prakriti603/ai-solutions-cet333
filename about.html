Who We Are

About AI-Solutions

A passionate start-up from Sunderland on a mission to make AI accessible, affordable, and impactful for businesses everywhere.

Our Mission

Innovate, Promote & Deliver the Future of Work

AI-Solutions was founded with a clear purpose — to bridge the gap between cutting-edge artificial intelligence and the everyday challenges businesses face. We believe that every organisation, regardless of size, deserves access to smart, affordable AI tools.

Based in Sunderland, UK, our team of engineers, designers, and AI specialists work collaboratively to build solutions that genuinely make a difference — from automating repetitive tasks to transforming the entire digital employee experience.

Our ambition is global. We are actively expanding into new industries and markets, determined to make AI a standard part of how people work around the world.
